Me & Roboco 2020 - Present

Aug | 2023

Writer Sayuri Ooba
Publisher Viz Media

"Me & Roboco" is a heartwarming manga centered around Bondo Kenichi, a high school student, and his unexpected companion, Roboco, a humanoid AI robot designed for chores. The story follows their endearing adventures and Roboco's humorous attempts to grasp human emotions and society. Balancing slice-of-life with science fiction, the manga explores their evolving relationship, highlighting themes of friendship, acceptance, and the interplay between humanity and technology. With a blend of laughter and touching moments, "Me & Roboco" offers an engaging and insightful read.
